About Me

I am a dedicated Computer Engineer with a specialization in Computer Engineering and a Masters degree in Information Management (MSc. IT). As a data science enthusiast, I am committed to engineering excellence and comprehensive software practices. My experience spans the development of enterprise applications, where I leverage a diverse set of technologies and methodologies.

Technical Expertise

  • API Development: Proficient in creating and integrating REST APIs, as well as third-party API integrations.
  • Distributed Systems and Big Data: Experienced in working with Apache Spark, Hadoop, and Oozie to manage and analyze large datasets.
  • Event-Driven Design: Skilled in implementing event-driven architectures using Apache Kafka to enhance system scalability and responsiveness.
  • Service-Oriented Architecture and Microservices: Adept in developing scalable applications using Java and Spring Boot.
  • Frontend and Backend Development: Proficient in Angular and Node.js, with aspirations to excel as a Full Stack Developer.
  • Database Management: Competent in writing efficient database scripts in PL/SQL, T-SQL, and PGSQL.
  • DevOps Practices: Familiar with CI/CD tools such as Jenkins and Bamboo, containerization with Docker, and orchestration using Kubernetes and Helm.

In addition to my technical skills, I excel in communication and presentation. I am adept at conveying complex technical concepts to diverse audiences, ensuring clarity and understanding among stakeholders. My collaborative approach fosters effective teamwork, enabling me to work seamlessly with cross-functional teams to achieve project goals. I also take pride in mentoring peers and sharing knowledge, which enhances team dynamics and drives innovation.

I have a strong passion for exploring new technology paradigms and continuously seek opportunities to advance my skills in emerging fields. I look forward to connecting and collaborating on innovative projects that drive technological advancement.
